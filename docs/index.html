About Langford Log

Langford Log is a professional mobile application designed specifically for employees at Langford Mechanical & Sheet Metal, INC. The app enables construction foremen and field personnel to efficiently submit detailed daily job logs, track employee hours, and maintain comprehensive project documentation with photo evidence.

This app is exclusively for authorized Langford Mechanical & Sheet Metal employees. All users must be registered and approved by company administrators to access the system.
