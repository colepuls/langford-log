Privacy Policy

Langford Log Mobile Application

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Langford Mechanical & Sheetmetal, INC. ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Langford Log mobile application (the "App"). This App is designed exclusively for authorized employees of Langford Mechanical & Sheetmetal, INC. for daily job tracking and reporting purposes.

Important: This App is intended solely for business use by authorized company employees. All data collected is used exclusively for legitimate business operations including payroll, project management, and regulatory compliance.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication credentials
  • Job Log Data: Daily work logs, employee hours, job numbers, task descriptions
  • Employee Information: Names and work hours of crew members
  • Photos: Images of work progress and job sites
  • Location Context: Job site information you manually enter

Information Automatically Collected

  • Authentication Data: Login sessions and access tokens via Firebase Authentication
  • App Usage Data: Feature usage and app performance metrics via Google Analytics
  • Technical Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Error Reports: Crash logs and performance data to improve app functionality

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Firebase (Google LLC)

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user login and account security
  • Firestore Database: Stores foremen and employee data
  • Google Analytics: Tracks app usage for performance improvement

Google Privacy Policy

Expo Platform

  • App development and deployment platform
  • May collect performance and crash analytics

Expo Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Information

  • Business Operations: Process daily logs, track employee hours, and manage project documentation
  • Payroll Processing: Calculate work hours and prepare payroll records
  • Project Management: Monitor job progress and maintain project records
  • Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements for construction projects
  • Communication: Send daily log reports via email to management
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality
  • Security: Maintain data security and prevent unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud Storage: Data is stored securely using Firebase/Google Cloud services
  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Control: Only authorized company personnel can access your data
  • Photo Processing: Photos are processed locally and sent via secure email
  • Data Retention: Business records are retained according to company policy and legal requirements

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Internal Business Use: With authorized company management and administrative staff
  • Service Providers: With third-party services (Firebase, email providers) necessary for app functionality
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Business Protection: To protect company rights, property, or safety

Your Rights

As a user of our App, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Account Management: Update your account information
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Questions: Contact us with any privacy-related concerns
Note: As this is a business application for company operations, certain data retention and access limitations may apply based on business needs and legal requirements.

Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for business use by company employees and is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, when appropriate, providing additional notice through the App or email.
